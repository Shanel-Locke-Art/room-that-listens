About This Room
You move through a small space. You choose what to touch. The machine watches what you notice, and makes a poem from the order of your attention.
Neither of you is the author alone.
THREE MOVEMENTS
First — Wander. Touch what draws you. The room is listening.
Second — The room asks you to follow a pattern. Some things are hidden. Finding them deepens what can be said.
Third — The room has chosen a theme. Find what belongs to it. Then return to the door.
