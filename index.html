Controls
Move: WASD or Arrows
Interact: E
Zoom: Mouse Wheel
Close: Esc
Speak Final: Space
Find Door: Q
Restart: R

How to Play
This is a collaboration between you and the computer. You do the noticing and choosing. The computer does the remembering and remixing.
Why a room? A room is a small universe with rules. It is a container for randomness. Each object is a different kind of language.
THE THREE ACTS
Act I — Explore the room and interact with objects to collect lines for the poem.
Act II — Follow the required object sequence and gather hidden SIG.
Act III — Revisit the objects the room asks for, then return to the door to seal the final poem.
