Move through the room. Choose at least two objects, then the door. You and the computer will finish the poem together. 

  


  

    

      
Controls

      
Move: WASD or Arrows

      
Interact: E

      
Zoom: Mouse Wheel

      
Close: Esc

      
Speak Final: Space

      
Restart: R

    

  






  

    
How to Play


    

      This is a collaboration between you and the computer.
      You do the noticing and choosing.
      The computer does the remembering and remixing.
    


    

      Why a room?
      A room is a small universe with rules.
      It is a container for randomness.
      Each object is a different kind of language.
    


    

      Interact with at least two objects.
      Each one changes the pattern and the poem.
      When you are ready, interact with the door.
      The door seals your collaboration into a final version.
